Cherry Limeade Vodka Popsicles

A fresh pick-me-up for all occasion!

Try not to get too drunk with this one

Ingredients

  • 2 cups limeade
  • 1 cup cherry vodka
  • a quarter cup finely chopped fresh cherries

Step(s)

  1. Stir together limeade, cherry vodka, and cherries. Pour into ice pop molds and freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
  2. Ta-da!
