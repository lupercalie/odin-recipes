Caramel Frappuccino

Totally not a copycat from Starbucks

The perfect drink to accompany your daily tasks. Please don't sue us.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of ice
  • 1 cup strongly brewed black coffee, cooled
  • 1 cup low fat milk
  • half cup of caramel sauce
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Blend ice, coffee, milk, caramel sauce, and sugar together in a blender on high speed until smooth.
  2. Pour into two 16-ounce glasses.
  3. That's it.
  4. Might as well add some whipped cream while you're at it.
